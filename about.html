About Mshengu Hanson

Engineering-led. Service-driven.

We deliver practical engineering support with consistency, safety, and real-world execution.

Background

Mshengu Hanson is a 100% Black owned company, originally formed in 2013. The company is proudly South African and is motivated by its intention to provide high quality service and excellence.

We are led by experienced and hands-on engineers and technicians with a wide range of skills in the built and technical environment. We pride ourselves on employing top technical resources to provide the best possible service for our clients.

Vision

To be the business technical support service provider of choice, known and respected for our ability to consistently deliver exceptional value through service excellence.

Mission

To provide sustainable and appropriate technical solutions with a positive social and environmental impact, therefore ensuring value for all stakeholders.

Culture

The company is an equal opportunity employer, committed to the development of historically disadvantaged persons to the highest level, in terms of professional training and equity in the company.
